\section*{Other useful resources}
The following additional resources may be consulted as background and additional reading where useful.
SOST71032 focuses on statistical modelling of network data.
Some of the following resources describe more foundational network analysis methods, such as descriptive and exploratory measures, which can often aid the analysis.
In particular, I recommend consulting \citet{rawlings2023integrating} as a good resource on descriptive and exploratory analysis.

\begin{itemize}
	\item \citet{rawlings2023integrating}
	\item \citet{borgatti2024analyzing}
	\item \citet{kolaczyk2020statistical}
	\item \citet{wasserman1994social}
\end{itemize}
The online reading list contains a few additional textbooks that may be of interest.
